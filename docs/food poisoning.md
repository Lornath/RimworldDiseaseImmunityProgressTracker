**Symptoms and progress**

Food poisoning lasts 24 hours with no after-effects. It has 3 stages: the first unpleasant 4 hours, followed by a crippling 16 hours, and finally an unpleasant 4 hours largely similar to the first stage, as described in the table below.

| Stage | Begins at | Symptoms |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Food poisoning (initial)** | 0–4 hours from onset | [Vomiting](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Vomiting) (*MTB of 0.3 days*) Some [pain](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Pain) (+20%) Impaired [consciousness](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Consciousness) (×60%) Impaired [moving](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Moving) (×80%) Impaired [manipulation](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Manipulation) (×90%) Reduced [blood filtration](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Blood_filtration) (×95%) Slower [eating](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Eating_speed) (×50%) |
| **Food poisoning (major)** | 4–20 hours from onset | [Vomiting](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Vomiting) (*MTB of 0.2 days*) Significant [pain](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Pain) (+40%) Impaired [consciousness](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Consciousness) (×50%) Impaired [moving](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Moving) (×50%) Impaired [manipulation](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Manipulation) (×80%) Reduced [blood filtration](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Blood_filtration) (×85%) Much slower [eating](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Eating_speed) (×30%) |
| **Food poisoning (recovering)** | 20–24 hours from onset | [Vomiting](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Vomiting) (*MTB of 0.4 days*) Some [pain](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Pain) (+20%) Impaired [consciousness](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Consciousness) (×60%) Impaired [moving](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Moving) (×80%) Impaired [manipulation](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Manipulation) (×90%) Reduced [blood filtration](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Blood_filtration) (×95%) Slower [eating](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Eating_speed) (×50%) |
