* ### **Artery blockage**

* A blockage in one of the critical arteries in the [heart](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Heart). Heart artery blockages randomly induce [heart attacks](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Ailments#Heart_attack). Artery blockages can be treated by replacing the heart with a [natural](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Heart), [prosthetic](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Prosthetic_heart) or [bionic](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Bionic_heart) replacement, with a [healer mech serum](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Healer_mech_serum), with [luciferium](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Luciferium), or with the use of [biosculpter pod](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Biosculpter_pod)'s bioregeneration cycle.  
* Artery blockages progress by a base of 0.0007 per day, multiplied by a random factor between 0.5 \- 3\. This means that artery blockages can take anywhere from 7.9 to 47.6 in-game years from onset to become fatal on its own.  
* Artery blockages can be treated by replacing the heart.

| Stage | Begins at | Symptoms |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Artery blockage (minor)** | ≥0% severity | \-5% part efficiency [Heart attack](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Ailments#Heart_attack) (*MTB of 300 days*) |
| **Artery blockage (minor)** | ≥20% severity | \-10% part efficiency [Heart attack](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Ailments#Heart_attack) (*MTB of 200 days*) |
| **Artery blockage (major)** | ≥40% severity | \-15% part efficiency [Heart attack](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Ailments#Heart_attack) (*MTB of 100 days*) |
| **Artery blockage (major)** | ≥60% severity | \-35% part efficiency [Heart attack](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Ailments#Heart_attack) (*MTB of 60 days*) |
| **Artery blockage (extreme)** | ≥90% severity | \-60% part efficiency [Heart attack](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Ailments#Heart_attack) (*MTB of 30 days*) |
| **Artery blockage (extreme)** | 100% severity | [Death](https://rimworldwiki.com/wiki/Death) |

* A human pawn must be at least 20 years old to get an artery blockage, meaning it can first occur at their 21st birthday
